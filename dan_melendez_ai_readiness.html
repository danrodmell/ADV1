The Framework
01
Axis One

AI for Product

Can your team build AI into what you ship? Engineering density, technical stack, and strategic intent to develop and maintain AI-powered features — measured externally and confirmed internally.

02
Axis Two

AI for Operations

Can your company automate its own internal processes with AI? Most high-performing product teams are still running their operations on spreadsheets and Slack threads. This is where margins go to die.

Classifications
